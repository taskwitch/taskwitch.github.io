My Impressions on AP

I want to share my impressions of AP, as a student who finished all the STEM subjects in AP and got ten 5’s during high school. What is AP? AP stands for Advanced Placement. Developed by the College Board, AP is a set of standardized, pre-college1 level courses that authorized high schools can offer. Taking an AP course usually involves two phases: first, you take the course at your high school, do homework, and sit exams that count toward your high school GPA; second, you sit the AP Exam at your designated test center in May, a test that typically runs three hours.
